Wynwood is an up and coming neighborhood, very colorful and the fact that there’s a lot of artist expression and it was named one of the coolest hipster neighborhoods in the USA. Exactly because it “SAN PAUL GALLERY urban art “ opened the doors in the bustling Art District in Miami on October 2014. The gallery focused on young and already established worldwide artists of street art, creating an interchange between diverse forms of cultural expression. The space offers a program of temporary performances and exhibitions to promote and consolidate the artists in the American art market. The artists show their works on canvas, drawings, photos and sculptures. With intense programming the San Paul Gallery exhibitions complement each other, and also promote a lively movement in the space on event days.
